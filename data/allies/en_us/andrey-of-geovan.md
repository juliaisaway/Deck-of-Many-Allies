# Andrey of Gèovan

> A member of a hidden order, he is dedicated to eliminating threats that should not exist.

**Track:** Whenever an ally deals damage to an adversary, place 1 token on this card.

**Hunter’s Hook:** Spend 3 tokens to make an Agility Roll against a target within Far range. On a success, Andrey pulls the target directly into Melee range.

**Precise Strike:** Spend 3 tokens to have Andrey deal 2d8 physical damage to an adversary within Melee range.
