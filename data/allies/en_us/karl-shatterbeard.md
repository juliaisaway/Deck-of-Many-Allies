# Karl Shatterbeard

> A veteran of the slopes, he believes a good shot solves what words cannot.

**Opportunist:** Whenever an ally fails an attack roll, place 1 token on this card.

**Hunter’s Lasso:** Spend 3 tokens to temporarily restrain a target within Very Close range. A Restrained target is also Vulnerable, but Karl must remain within Very Close range of them.

**Sweeping Shot:** Spend 2 tokens to have Karl fire in a cone. Roll 3d6 and distribute the damage among all adversaries within Very Close range in front of him.
