# Coltan Ganzorig

> With calloused hands and a fiery spirit, he fights both to survive and for those he loves.

**Triumph:** Whenever an adversary is defeated, place 1 token on this card.

**Rebel Strike:** Spend 1 token to have Coltan deal 2d8+3 physical damage to an adversary within Melee range. If that adversary is **Restrained**, deal an additional 1d8 damage.

**Inspire the Rebellion:** Spend 3 tokens for Coltan to grant 1d4 to each ally within Very Close range. An ally can roll this die and add the result to an action roll, reaction roll, or damage roll. Unused dice are lost at the end of the scene.
