# Gilbert Balzath

> A wanderer of uncertain origins, always ready with his multi-shot firearm.

**Inspired:** Whenever an ally rolls with Hope on their Duality Dice, place 1 token on this card.

**Quick Shot:** Spend 1 token to have Gilbert deal 2d8 physical damage to an adversary within Close range.

**Unload!:** Once per rest, you may spend all tokens on this card to have Gilbert deal 1d6 physical damage to a single adversary within Close range for each token removed.
