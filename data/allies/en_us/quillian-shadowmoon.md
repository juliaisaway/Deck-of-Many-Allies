# Quillian Shadowmoon

> A renowned traveling bard who brings his music to every corner he visits.

**Channel:** Whenever this ally gains the spotlight, place 1 token on this card.

**Captivate the Audience:** Spend 1 token to have Quillian temporarily Distract a target within Close range, reducing that target’s Difficulty by −2. You may spend 3 additional tokens for each extra target you wish to Distract in this way.
