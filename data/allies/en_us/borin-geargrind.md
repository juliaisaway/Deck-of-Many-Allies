# Borin Geargrind

> Once he broke rocks. Now he breaks anything that dares stand in his way.

**Triumph:** Whenever an adversary is defeated, place 1 token on this card.

**Spinning Barrage:** If Borin is not Overheated, spend 3 tokens to attack a target within Far range, dealing 2d6 physical damage. You may continue firing. For each additional shot, roll 1d6 and add the result to the damage. If you roll a 1 or 2, Borin becomes Overheated and you stop rolling.

**Vent the Drum:** Spend 1 token to remove the Overheated condition from Borin.
