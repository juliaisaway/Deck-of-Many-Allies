# Torgin Brimbleblast

> If it works, it’s genius. If it doesn’t… well, it’s still impressive.

**Pressured:** Whenever an ally rolls with Fear on their Duality Dice, place 1 token on this card.

**Unstable Charge:** Spend 3 tokens to have Torgin throw an explosive at an adversary within Far range, dealing 2d8 physical damage. After the attack, roll 1d6:

- 1: the adversary immediately takes an action  
- 2–4: nothing happens  
- 5–6: the adversary becomes Vulnerable temporarily and place 1 token on this card.
