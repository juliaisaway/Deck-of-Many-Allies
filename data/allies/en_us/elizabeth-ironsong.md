# Elizabeth Ironsong

> A young scholar devoted to recording the stories of the world through the eyes of those who live them.

**Witness (2):** Whenever an ally marks 2 or more Hit Points, place 1 token on this card.

**Strategic Notes:** Spend 1 token for Elizabeth to grant advantage on an ally’s next attack roll.
