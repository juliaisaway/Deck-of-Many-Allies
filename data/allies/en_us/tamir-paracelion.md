# Tamir Paracelion

> An eccentric alchemist who claims to be perfecting “the ultimate formula of controlled combustion.”

**Inspired:** Whenever an ally rolls with Hope on their Duality Dice, place 1 token on this card.

**Alchemical Bomb:** Spend 3 tokens to have Tamir throw an explosive at a target within Far range, dealing 1d20+3 physical damage.

**Refreshing Tonic:** Spend 3 tokens to have Tamir clear 1 Hit Point from an ally within Melee range.
